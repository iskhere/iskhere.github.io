Story


"I CAN'T PHOTOGRAPH ANYTHING WITHOUT A CITY."

I am an amateur photographer specializing in street photography. My story began when I first read about Daido Moriyama and bought my Ricoh GR few years ago. I enjoy wandaring in cities and being in spontaneous and oscure situations. There might or might not be a story behind ambiguous people or places on streets, and that is the ambiguity therein that tempts me to press the button and make that moment to be part of my visual statement - how we preceive the oscurity of being.
